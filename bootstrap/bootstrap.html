Wendys

I usually order a Baconator with Baconator Fries or Spicy Chicken Nuggets. Some Wendys has a special 50-Nugget Party Pack and it's quite a steal.

Chipotle

At Chipotle I get a burrito with double chicken, double rice, cheese, tomatillo red-chili salsa, and sour cream.

Popeyes

I grab a bucket of 8-piece dark meat chicken to eat for lunch for a couple days. Some Popeyes have a Tuesday special, and the one in Normal has one.

McDonalds

Sometimes I get chicken nuggets, sometimes I get double a quarter-pounder with cheese, sometimes I get both.

Buffalo Wild Wings

The only time I really get Bdubds is on Thursdays when it's BOGO Boneless. My favorite sauce to get is Parmesan Garlic, and then depending on my mood my second sauce is spicy, whether it's medium or hot or wild.

Taco Bell

Taco Bell has lots of options but I always go for the Beefy 5-Layer burrito and I would add extra cheese sauce. Whenever Taco Bell has Nacho Fries I will always order them because they are very tasty.
