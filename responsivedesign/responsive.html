The Eternals

Ajak Druig Gilgamesh Ikaris Kingo Makkari Phastos Sersi Sprite Thena

The Eternal Ajak is the spiritual leader of the group, guiding the Eternals on their mission from their home planet to Earth to defend humankind from the predatorial Deviants.

Druig has the powerful ability to control the minds of humans with his cosmic energy, which at times makes it difficult to determine if he’s friend or foe.

The powerful Eternal Gilgamesh is a fierce warrior remembered throughout history for his legendary battles.

The powerful Eternal Ikaris is the moral, charismatic tactician of the group, tasked with keeping his team and humanity safe.

The boisterous Eternal Kingo, enamored with the idea of fame, has taken the most advantage of his centuries-long time on Earth.

With her cosmic-powered super-speed, Makkari scouts planets for the Eternals, a group of ancient, immortal beings working on behalf of the Celestials.

The genius Phastos has nudged humanity’s technological advancements forward throughout the centuries while shielding his own brilliance.

The humankind-loving Eternal Sersi delights in her mission stationed on Earth; she’s as happy working as a museum curator as she is saving humanity.

The Eternal Sprite is the grandiose storyteller of the group who just so happens to possess the appearance of a 13-year-old girl.

The fierce warrior Thena is more comfortable in battle than anywhere else.
